Provide Feedback

In most of the cases you can provide feedback for the item recommended by the CROSSMINER.
Anywhere you see the following icon, it means that there is a possibility to provide your feedback for the CROSSMINER server.

Feedback icon

By clicking on this icon the system opens the Feedback dialog where you can leave your rating and your opinion concerning the given recommended item.

Feedback dialog

You can provide a numerical rating from 1 to 5 by clicking on the stars. The more the number of the opaque stars the better is the rating.
Furthermore, you can provide a text-based rating by filling the textbox in the bottom of the dialog.
